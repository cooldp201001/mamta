
1.what is full stack development? 

Ans) Full Stack Development refers to the development of both front end (client side) and back end (server side) portions of web application.

2. Who is full stack developer? 
A full stack developer is a professional who has the knowledge and skills to work on both the front end and back end of a web application or software project. They are proficient in multiple technologies and can handle various aspects of development, including client-side programming, server-side programming, and database management.
